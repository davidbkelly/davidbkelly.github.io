This work presents the development of a coupled three-dimensional finite-volume left ventricular model and a zero-dimensional lumped parameter circulation implemented in OpenFOAM. The framework enables dynamic exchange of pressure, flow, and volume between the cardiac and circulatory domains, providing physiologically consistent boundary conditions for high-fidelity ventricular simulations.
